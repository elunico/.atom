Evolving Steering Behaviors

This sketch is an elaboration on a Coding Challenge (#69) done on the Coding Train by Dan Shiffman. I began with the code from the end of part 4 and added several things. For a list of things that I changed, see this page.

  • Altruism: Each Vehicle can see other Vehicles and may seek them out. When encountering another Vehicle, a Vehicle may choose to share some of its health (food) with that Vehicle. Both the distance at which a Vehicle can be seen and the willingness to share food are integrated into the DNA of the Vehicles and can evolve.
